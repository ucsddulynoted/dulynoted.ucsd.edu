About Us

Duly Noted is the seventh of nine a cappella groups found on campus at University of California, San Diego. Founded in Fall of 2012, Duly Noted has made strides to get to where they are today. Together, the members of Duly Noted sing and perform, united by a love of music.

Comprised of nearly 20 talented students from UCSD, Duly Noted sings a diverse repertoire of music. Each year, the group sings new arrangements composed by our members. Duly Noted also hosts three yearly concerts, perform at on-campus events, attend festivals, and compete at the International Championship of Collegiate A Cappella.

Members not only share a love for singing, but also for each other.
